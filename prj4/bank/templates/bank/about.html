About Us


At USF Online Banking, we’re here to help the University of South Florida’s faculty, staff, students, alumni association members and their families achieve financial success. The mighty Bull, the University of South Florida’s mascot, is defined by strength, stability, power and support. And these are the principles we’ve put into practice since 1959.

With more than 70,000 members and $1+ billion in assets, robust online and mobile banking, nine branch locations and thousands of shared branches and fee-free ATMs nationwide, we’re here when you need us.
